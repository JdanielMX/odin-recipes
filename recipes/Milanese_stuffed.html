Milanese stuffed with ham and cheese

In this recipe the breasts are breaded and the cheese inside is definitely gratinated, in such a way that when you bite into it, the sensation is absolutely exquisite, because you feel how the cheese, ham, chicken and panko explode in your mouth and one like diner surrenders to her.

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts
  • 10 slices of cheese for sandwiches
  • 10 large or 20 small slices of ham
  • 2 eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tin of chipotle peppers
  • 1 cup chicken broth (with bouillon powder)

Recipe instructions

  1. Fillet the chicken breasts. Cut fillets as thin as you can with the help of a sharp knife. You can ask the butcher to make it for you.
  2. Prepare ham and cheese packages
  3. Prepare the cordon bleu . Tuck each fillet between two plastic wrap and pound to squash until very thin.
  4. Place a small packet of ham and cheese on top of each fillet and roll up on itself.
  5. Season the stuffed breasts with salt to taste. Pass each breast through beaten egg and breadcrumbs.
  6. Pass each breast through the breadcrumbs, then through the well beaten egg, and again through the breadcrumbs. Insist well with the breadcrumbs on the edges so that they do not open when frying.
  7. Serve the breasts on a plate. Consider that later you will add sauce.
  8. In the blender, add the chipotles, milk, chicken broth and grind for a couple of minutes until the texture of the sauce is fine.
  9. Heat the sauce in a pan for about 5 or 10 minutes .
  10. Bathe the breasts with the sauce or serve the mixture separately.
